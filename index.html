Private Internet Infrastructure

Here are some infrastructure that people use themselves, mostly the are terminals od the internet or be used like a receiver.

Powerline adapter

This adapter is an advance of normal network cable. It has two terminals on is connected with the router, another one is connected with computer, both of the them are plug in the power supply in that it can transport signal by the electrical circuit.

Router

Router is a basic equipment for every home to transmit wireless signals. It greatly facilitates people's live. It makes the wifi signal available at the certain place and reduce the inconvenience caused by the network cable.

Phone

Phone is the daily tool,that people can not live without it. Phone is the terminal for internet system and is where the signals come in.
